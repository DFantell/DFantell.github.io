Hello, Welcome to a page all about me!


My name is David and I created this page as a pre-req to a coding bootcamp called #VETSWHOCODE. Currently, I am a Senior Recruiting Consultant for 3rd party agency that works closely with various Tech Companies in the San Francisco Bay Area.


I am still in the Army Reserves but was Active Duty Army for about 10 Years prior. During that time, I served as an Airborne Infantryman and a Recruiter. I deployed to Afghanistan and reached the rank of Staff Sergeant.


I have always had an interest in Computers and Technology and find ways to “Automate The Boring Stuff” anyway that I can. When I started recruiting for Developers and Engineers, I decided it would be in my best interest to learn a little about what I was recruiting for. I read an article called "What is Code" by: Paul Ford and proceeded to start my coding journey.


I started teaching myself Python by watching tutorials and YouTube videos and decided to create a GUI using Python TKinter. I adapted this program to collect and analyze recruiting metrics and candidate data. I would input this data and analyze t with Pandas then export to excel, PDF or HTML to create reports to present to clients and senior leadership. After this was “completed: I pivoted to converting it to a Web Web App utilizing Python Flask, HTML, CSS and JavaScript- this is still a work in progress.


During this time, I decided I wanted to pursue a career in coding and started looking into more formal training to get started. This is my introductory story.


Hope you enjoy it.

Career
